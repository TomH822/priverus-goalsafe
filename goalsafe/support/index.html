GoalSafe Support

Need help with GoalSafe? This page covers common support topics and how to contact us.

GoalSafe is a manual-entry iPhone savings planning app. It does not connect to your bank and does not import transactions automatically. If your GoalSafe balance does not match your real-world bank balance, use the Reconciliation feature to compare and resolve the difference.

1. Getting Started

Use the Setup Wizard to enter your starting balance, planned deposits, and recurring obligations. GoalSafe uses this information to calculate your Levelized Deposit and show future projections.

2. Levelized Deposit

The Levelized Deposit is the steady per-deposit amount GoalSafe calculates from your planned obligations across the planning horizon. Your existing savings balance does not reduce this number; existing savings are handled separately in your fund allocation and catch-up decisions.

3. Manual Ledger

GoalSafe’s Ledger is manual-entry by design. Add deposits, withdrawals, transfers, and reconciliation entries yourself. GoalSafe does not connect to your bank.

4. Remove Ads

GoalSafe includes an optional one-time Remove Ads purchase. If you already purchased Remove Ads, use Restore Purchases in Settings to restore your entitlement.

5. Reconciliation

If your real-world bank balance differs from GoalSafe’s modeled balance, use Reconciliation to identify the difference and decide how to resolve it.

6. Privacy

GoalSafe stores your manually entered planning data locally on your device and does not request bank login credentials. See the Privacy Policy for more information: https://priverus.ai/goalsafe/privacy/

7. Contact

For support, email: support@priverus.ai

Please include:

  • your device model;
  • your iOS version;
  • your GoalSafe app version;
  • a clear description of the issue;
  • screenshots if helpful, but do not include sensitive financial details unless necessary.
