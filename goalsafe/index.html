Built for deliberate savings

GoalSafe is built for people who want a clear, private, and deliberate way to manage savings for known future expenses. Instead of guessing how much to set aside, GoalSafe computes a Levelized Deposit: one steady per-deposit number based on the amount, timing, and escalation of your planned obligations across a multi-year horizon.

Your existing savings balance does not reduce or distort that number. What you already have is handled separately and transparently through GoalSafe's fund allocation, dashboard, projections, ledger, and reconciliation features.

GoalSafe is manual-entry by design. It does not connect to your bank, does not request your bank login, and does not import account transactions. You enter your starting balance, planned obligations, deposits, and ledger activity yourself.
