SaveSure Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 4, 2026

SaveSure is an iPhone savings planning app published by Thomas Hamilton dba Priverus. This Privacy Policy explains how SaveSure handles information. SaveSure is designed as a manual-entry, on-device planning tool. It does not connect to banks or financial institutions, does not request bank login credentials, and does not create a SaveSure account.

1. Information You Enter in SaveSure

SaveSure allows you to manually enter information such as:

  • starting balances;
  • planned deposits;
  • planned expenses and withdrawals;
  • Reserve Fund names, targets, and projected calls;
  • ledger transactions;
  • reconciliation amounts;
  • optional notes or memo text.

This information is used by SaveSure to calculate and display your savings plan, projected balances, fund breakdowns, ledger, and reconciliation information.

SaveSure stores this app data locally on your device. SaveSure does not operate a server account system for this data and does not use bank connectivity.

2. No Bank Connectivity

SaveSure does not connect to your bank or financial institution. It does not request, collect, or store bank login credentials. You are responsible for entering and updating your own information.

3. In-App Purchase

SaveSure offers an optional one-time in-app purchase called “Remove Ads.” Purchases and restores are handled by Apple’s App Store and StoreKit. Priverus does not receive or store your payment card number or Apple account credentials.

SaveSure receives purchase-entitlement information from Apple so the app can determine whether to show or hide banner ads.

4. Advertising

SaveSure may show banner ads to users who have not purchased Remove Ads. Ads are provided through Google Mobile Ads / AdMob.

For Release 1, SaveSure is intended to use non-personalized banner advertising and is available in the United States only.

Google Mobile Ads may collect or process certain information to provide and measure ads, such as device identifiers and advertising-related data. The exact data handled by Google may depend on Google’s SDK, Apple platform rules, device settings, and Google’s policies.

You can learn more about Google’s advertising technologies and privacy practices from Google’s published privacy materials.

5. App Store Privacy Labels

SaveSure’s App Store privacy disclosures are intended to reflect both SaveSure’s own practices and the practices of third-party software included in the app, including Google Mobile Ads. If the app’s data practices or third-party SDK behavior changes, the App Store privacy disclosures will be updated as required.

6. Data Not Collected by SaveSure Servers

SaveSure does not operate a user-account server for your manually entered financial planning data. SaveSure does not sell your manually entered financial planning data.

7. Device Storage and Backups

SaveSure data is stored locally on your device. Depending on your Apple device settings, local app data may be included in device backups managed by Apple, such as iCloud or computer backups. Those backup systems are controlled by Apple and your device settings.

8. Children

SaveSure is not directed to children. SaveSure is a personal finance planning tool intended for users who manage their own savings and obligations.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If the policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
